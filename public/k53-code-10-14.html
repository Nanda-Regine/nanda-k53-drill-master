Frequently asked questions

What speed limit applies to a heavy goods vehicle?

A goods vehicle with a GVM over 9 000 kg is limited to 80 km/h on all roads, including freeways, because of its longer braking distance.

What is the following distance for a heavy vehicle?

At least 3 seconds behind the vehicle in front — one second more than a light vehicle — and more in poor conditions.

Which licence code do I need for a truck?

Code C1/C for rigid heavy vehicles and EC1/EC for articulated/extra-heavy combinations. Carrying goods or passengers for reward also needs a PrDP.
