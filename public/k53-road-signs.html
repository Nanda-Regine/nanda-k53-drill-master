Frequently asked questions

How many road signs are on the K53 test?

The Code 8 learner’s test has 17 road-sign questions out of 68; the Code 1 (motorcycle) test has 13 of 40. You need 75% in the signs section to pass.

What are the categories of South African road signs?

Regulatory (control, command, prohibition, reservation, de-restriction), warning, guidance and temporary signs. K53 Drill Master lets you drill each category separately.

Are the sign images the real ones?

Yes — every sign is rendered crisp from the official Department of Transport road-traffic-signs chart, so what you practise is what you’ll see.
