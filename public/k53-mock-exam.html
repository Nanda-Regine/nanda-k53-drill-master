Frequently asked questions

How many questions is the K53 learner’s test?

The Code 8 test is 68 questions: 17 signs, 24 rules and 27 vehicle controls. You must score at least 75% in each of the three sections.

What happens if I fail one section?

You fail the whole test even if your total is high — each section is marked separately at 75%. The mock exam shows your section scores so you know exactly where to focus.
